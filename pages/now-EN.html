Life

I started living in Barcelona about a month ago, and I've been lazy about finding a pool for me, so I started running again. I am reaching 10k this weekend, and I wish to participate in a marathon this year.

What I am working on

I'm still working as a software developer in the Center for the Advanced Research on Education or (CIAE). I'm doing mostly backend development, now remotely.

I'm still finishing details on the web application we use to display custom maps and demographics about the schools situation in Santiago de Chile. I struggled a lot with the deployment, and I've learnt so much by doing so.

What I am learning

  • I dropped dataquest's data engineering path. I don't know yet if I've wasted money, I just feel it doesn't cut it for me. Maybe I'm past that point? I don't know. I learn better from books.
  • I am halfway on Cesar Hidalgo's book about why information grows. I attended to a presentation he did during the launch of DataChile and it was really powerful.
  • I started reading Gödel, Escher, Bach: An Eternal Golden Braid by Douglas R. Hofstadter. I'm about 30% of it, and though I cannot say it has not been difficult, it has blown my mind several times. I want to finish it by the end of this year!

The future

I will start a Master in Research in Informatics in the Universidad Politécnica de Catalunya. I'm really excited about it! I am looking for something interesting to work on, I hope I will have better luck this year!
